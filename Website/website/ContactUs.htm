Contacts
 

The Octopus Music API is been developed by Leandro Costalonga(currently a PhD. Student of ICCMR), under supervision of Prof. Eduardo Miranda and Dr. John Matthias.

 For general queries, please e-mail leandro.costalonga at plymouth.ac.uk.

If you wish to receive news from the the current stage of development, please subscribe to octopusmusic-news@list.sourceforge.net (low traffic).

To report bugs, suggest features, get in touch with other users or any other type of support, please, go to the SouceForge Octopus Music API Project ( http://sourceforge.net/projects/octopusmusic/).

Thanks for you interest in helping us develop this research.
