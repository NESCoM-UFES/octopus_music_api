The Interdisciplinary Centre for Computer Music Research
 

The Interdisciplinary Centre for Computer Music Research team (ICCMR) is formed of scholars from different backgrounds and from different departments across the University of Plymouth(UK).

  • School of Computing, Communications and Electronics
  • Faculty of Education, Music
  • School of Media and Photography

We have close links with I-DAT and the Auditory Group at CTCN.

Computer Music is defined as the study of Music with Computer Science. We are chiefly interested in computational modelling of Music. We are developing new intelligent musical systems that will be able to evolve their own rules for musical composition and ability to interact with musicians and listeners in much more sophisticated ways than the present ones can do. We predict the emergence of new kinds of music content, most of which will be generated on the fly, requiring new modes of representation, access and interaction. New research topics such as Evolutionary Computer Music and Neuroscience of Music are emerging as natural progressions in Computer Music research.

For futher information visit the website: http://cmr.soc.plymouth.ac.uk/
